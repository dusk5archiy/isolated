In Yazi:

- To show help (key bindings), press `F1`.
- To open a new yazi tab, press `t`.
- To change directory, press `g<space>` then type a Windows path (`C:/users` for example).
- To show files' sizes, press `ms`.
- To open the current folder in File Explorer, press `;` and type `start .`.
- Press `CTRL+SHIFT+T` to open a new Wezterm tab based on the current yazi tab's current directory.
You can also use `CTRL+SHIFT+N` to open a new Wezterm window.
